Thesis

Print and Design Arts

A series of prints that provides a new perspective into the personal thoughts of an individual coping with schizophrenia.

The challenge was to persuade the viewer into seeing those living with schizophrenia as more than their disorder by eloquently demonstrating the need and benefit of empathy. Empathy provides a basis for understanding and enables self-awareness, growth, insight, and healing.
